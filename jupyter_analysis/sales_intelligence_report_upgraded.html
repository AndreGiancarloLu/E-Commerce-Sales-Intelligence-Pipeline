Executive Summary

The e-commerce platform generated a total revenue of $5,932,308, demonstrating strong recent momentum. May 2026 revenue reached $436,683, marking a significant 62.11% month-over-month growth, following 11.65% growth in April and 20.84% in March. Our top-performing categories are Outerwear & Coats, Jeans, and Sweaters, contributing $727,796, $672,257, and $452,904 respectively. Geographically, China, the United States, and Brasil are our leading markets, generating $2,012,751, $1,355,733, and $863,551 in revenue, with Search identified as our primary traffic driver.

Despite this positive revenue trend, customer retention presents a critical risk, with a high churn rate of 79.1% among customers who have placed at least one order. Only 16,696 out of 100,000 total customers have been active in the last 90 days, indicating a small engaged base. Furthermore, 19.9% of our total customer base has never placed an order. This creates a significant reliance on a small segment, as our top 10% of customers account for 50.7% of total revenue, posing a vulnerability if their engagement declines.

To mitigate churn and capitalize on recent growth, we have significant opportunities to enhance customer lifetime value. Implementing targeted retention campaigns for the 79.1% churned customers and conversion strategies for the 19.9% who never ordered could substantially expand our active customer base. We should also explore loyalty programs for our top 10% of customers to secure their continued high contribution. Further investment in optimizing our top categories and geographic markets, particularly China, the US, and Brasil, along with continued SEO/SEM efforts, will be crucial to sustaining and accelerating our positive revenue trajectory.
